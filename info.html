ABOUT
Multidisciplinary filmmaker and visual artist working across narrative film, photography, and graphic experimentation. I am drawn to intimate human moments and the quiet emotional currents that move beneath everyday life. My work blends storytelling with visual texture, using film, still images, and design to build soft, cinematic spaces.

Inspired by everyday textures, late-night city light and small, human gestures that feel larger than life.
